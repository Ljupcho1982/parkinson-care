🧠 Parkinson Care — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Parkinson Care ("the app") is a medication reminder and well-being tracker for people living with Parkinson's disease. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

None is sent to us or anyone else. The app has no account, no server, and no analytics. Everything you enter — your medications, schedules, dose history and well-being notes — is stored only on your device, in the app's local storage. We (the developer) cannot see it.

Data we share

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or health data. No third-party advertising or tracking SDKs are included. The only data that ever leaves your device is when you choose to use the in-app Export feature to save a JSON/CSV file or share it with your doctor.

Permissions and why they are used

  • Notifications / Alarms & reminders / Full-screen intent — to ring and show the alarm when a dose is due, even when the phone is locked.
  • Display over other apps ("Appear on top") — to show the full-screen alarm from the background.
  • Run at startup (boot) — to restore your scheduled alarms after the phone restarts.
  • Vibrate / Wake lock — to vibrate and wake the screen for the alarm.

These permissions are used solely to deliver medication reminders. They are not used to collect information.

Children

The app is intended for adults managing their medication and is not directed at children.

Medical disclaimer

The app helps you track and remember — it does not provide medical advice and is not a medical device. Never change your medication or schedule without consulting your doctor or neurologist.

Data deletion

You can delete all data at any time from within the app (History → "Clear all data"), or by uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data.
