# 🧠 Parkinson Care

A simple, private **medication & well-being tracker** for people living with Parkinson's disease.

- ✅ **Track every dose** — one tap to log a pill, with the exact time recorded
- ⏰ **On-schedule check** — see what's due now, what's overdue, and today's adherence %
- 🔔 **Full-screen voice alarm** — when a dose is due: giant letters, spoken voice (English / Macedonian), sound & vibration, with **I TOOK IT** / **SNOOZE**
- 📝 **Daily well-being diary** — tremor, stiffness, energy, sleep, "off" episodes, notes for your doctor
- 📋 **History + export** — JSON / CSV you can show your neurologist
- 📲 **Installable** (PWA) and **works offline**
- 🔒 **100% private** — all data stays on your device; nothing is uploaded

## Install on your phone (Android)
1. Open the live link in **Chrome**.
2. Menu **⋮ → Install app** (or *Add to Home screen*).
3. Tap **Enable alarms** and tap once anywhere to unlock voice/sound.
4. Add your pills in **My Pills**, then keep the app open or in the background.

> For the most reliable alarms: **Android Settings → Apps → Parkinson Care → Battery → Unrestricted.**

## ⚕️ Disclaimer
This app helps you **track and remember** — it does **not** give medical advice. Never change your
medication or schedule without talking to your doctor or neurologist.
